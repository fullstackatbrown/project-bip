Community Health
The Community Health Team researches and advocates for legislation to support public health efforts in Rhode Island.
Overall Purpose
Formerly the Older Adult Policy team, the Community Health team focuses on policies concerning public health issues in Rhode Island. Past projects include: Partnering with the AARP to research and advocate for legislation on pet adoptions for Rhode Island seniors; researching the potential for Meals on Wheels drivers to address senior isolation; and collaboration with researchers at Brown and Johns Hopkins to update a national report on adult day service regulations.
Short-Term Goals
1. Research the state of homelessness in Rhode Island and public health impacts of encampments
2. Publish a report on homelessness/encampment legislation in Rhode Island and policy suggestions to protect the rights of individuals experiencing homelessness
